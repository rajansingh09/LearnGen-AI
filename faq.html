General Questions

LearnGen.ai is an AI-powered learning platform that provides personalized learning paths, instant tutoring, and progress tracking to help students achieve their academic goals. Our platform adapts to each learner's unique needs, providing tailored content and recommendations.

Our AI Tutor uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to understand your questions, explain concepts in multiple ways, and provide step-by-step guidance in real-time. It learns from your interactions to provide increasingly personalized support.
